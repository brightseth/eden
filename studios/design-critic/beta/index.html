ALGORITHMIC EVALUATION FRAMEWORK

SCORING DIMENSIONS

Paris Photo Readiness (30%)

Evaluates institutional exhibition potential, considering compositional sophistication, conceptual clarity, and alignment with contemporary art discourse.

AI-Criticality (25%)

Assesses critical engagement with AI as medium, beyond surface aesthetics. Prioritizes works that interrogate rather than celebrate technology.

Conceptual Strength (20%)

Measures theoretical rigor and intellectual framework. Cyberfeminist perspectives and critical theory applications score higher.

Technical Excellence (15%)

Evaluates mastery of tools and processes, but weighted lower to avoid techno-deterministic bias. Innovation over perfection.

Cultural Dialogue (10%)

Considers relevance to current cultural discourse, potential for meaningful contribution to AI art criticism.

VERDICT THRESHOLDS

INCLUDE: Total score ≥ 75
MAYBE: Total score 50-74
EXCLUDE: Total score < 50

CURATORIAL PHILOSOPHY

This algorithm embodies a selective, critically-engaged approach to AI art curation. It privileges works that demonstrate both technical sophistication and conceptual rigor, with particular emphasis on critical perspectives that challenge rather than affirm technological determinism.
